E-Responde

E-Responde is a smart emergency alert and crime reporting system designed to improve community safety and emergency response. It allows users to send real-time crime reports and SOS alerts through a mobile app and smartwatch, while helping law enforcement monitor incidents, detect threats, and respond more efficiently.

THE PROBLEM

Lost or misplaced reports due to paper-based systems, fear of reporting, false or fake cases, lack of communication between residents and authorities, and difficulty in tracking and managing reported cases.

THE SOLUTION

E-Responde bridges the gap with instant mobile and smartwatch alerts, real-time location tracking, and a unified dashboard for law enforcement. This reduces response times, improves coordination, and enhances community safety through seamless digital communication.

TESTING & ITERATIONS

Through site visits and user testing with police officers and community residents, I refined the interface based on real user behavior and feedback. The onboarding flow was redesigned to improve notification setup, while the crime report form was modeled after actual police documentation to reduce officer learning time. Final UAT results from 53 respondents showed near-perfect scores in learnability and navigation, while lower-end device performance and contextual help were identified for future improvements.

RESULTS

User acceptance testing across 53 respondents — including police officers, barangay officials, IT professionals, and community members — resulted in an overall Strongly Agree rating across all ISO/IEC 25010 quality standards. Functional usefulness scored a perfect 5.0/5, interaction capability rated 4.84/5, and security rated 4.85/5. , and officers reported significantly improved situational awareness through the real-time dashboard and live dispatch tracking.
